Community

De Weegbree is a community of people with a vision for a feasible and workable alternative. Like many other people in the transition movement around the world, we believe that: for us, for our planet and everything that lives on it - it is nicer, better and healthier when we organize ourselves locally . In such a way that we grow our food locally, generate our energy needs locally in a decentralized manner, process our waste on site and purify our water.

Sustainability

At De Weegbree we try to become as self-sufficient as possible in order to provide for our basic needs. We realize that it will be difficult for us to provide for all our basic needs ourselves, space is too limited for this, but we can still go a long way by making optimal use of what we have. The things that we cannot make ourselves, we try to find locally in the first place. It is about (organic) basic ingredients with which we can make other things ourselves.

Creativity

We have displayed an incredible amount of creativity, resourcefulness and adaptability over the time that we have had more and more energy available, and there is no reason not to do the same as we start the energy descent. We work together and planning on time and taking action, it also looks like we can create a lifestyle that is more connected, fuller and more in tune with our environment than the whirlwind of oil addiction we find ourselves in today.

Permaculture

Permaculture is working with nature to make a better world for all of us. When we observe nature we see that there is a system of principles at work. Permaculture uses these principles to develop integrated systems that care for our needs for food, energy and community in a healthy and efficient way. Permaculture allows us to improve the quality and productivity of our individual lives, our society and our environment.

Permaculture is the design of systems for maximum output with minimal input. Permaculture provides us with the tools and processes that enable us to unite conceptual, material and strategic components in a 'pattern' or 'plan of action' that can be built and maintained with minimal resources.

Natural building

A natural building involves a range of building systems and materials that place major emphasis on sustainability. Ways of achieving sustainability through natural building focus on durability and the use of minimally processed, plentiful or renewable resources, as well as those that, while recycled or salvaged, produce healthy living environments and maintain indoor air quality. Natural building tends to rely on human labor, more than technology.

The basis of natural building is the need to lessen the environmental impact of buildings and other supporting systems, without sacrificing comfort or health. To be more sustainable, natural building uses primarily abundantly available, renewable, reused or recycled materials. The use of rapidly renewable materials is increasingly a focus.

Cultural activities

As a community we ourselves are in transition to a more feasible way of life for this time. We make our space available to neighbors, friends and other interested parties to come and taste and see, to participate or to organize something ourselves. Some transition initiatives that have already taken place here:

  • Film evenings (raising awareness)
  • Handicraft market (meeting, exchanging, learning)
  • People's kitchen
  • Repair Café
  • Seed exchange fair
  • Clothing exchange and clothing gift shop
  • Neighborhood parties (farm party, harvest party, experience party)
  • Organizing very practical courses: permaculture, growth trajectory self-sufficient life, crafts, natural beekeeping, inner transition...
